# 11_parent_aios_candidates

## Purpose

Holds recommendations that MAY be reusable beyond this subfolder. Nothing here is promoted to parent-AIOS truth; promotion happens only through the correct parent-AIOS validation process. Every file in this folder must state: DRAFT CANDIDATE - NOT PARENT-AIOS TRUTH.

## Accepted Assets

- Candidate assets recommended for wider reuse
- Justifications for why a candidate may generalise
- Files that clearly state: DRAFT CANDIDATE - NOT PARENT-AIOS TRUTH.

## Not Allowed

- Anything presented as approved parent-AIOS truth
- Promotion of an asset into parent AIOS from here
- Candidates with no source or evidence

## Source and Evidence Rule

Every important asset stored here must reference its source (where the truth came from) and its evidence (proof the work was done), with the evidence stored in `06_evidence` and linked by path. If an asset cannot point to a source and evidence, it is treated as not done and must not be relied upon.
